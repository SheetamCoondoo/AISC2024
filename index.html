About AISC 2024

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence & Sustainable Computing (AISC 2024) provides an environment where ideas are created, exchanged, and synthesized as researchers, practitioners, and educators present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, experiences, and challenges in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Sustainable Computing. The Conference will be held on July 11-13, 2024. AISC 2024 is being organized jointly by Department of Information Technology, B. P. Poddar Institute of Management & Technology, Kolkata & A. K. Choudhury School of Information Technology, University of Calcutta, India. The conference will include presentations of contributed papers, posters along with keynote lectures, and invited talks.
